About Taniti

Taniti is a beautiful Pacific island destination known for its pristine beaches, lush tropical forests, and vibrant local culture. Whether you're looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, Taniti offers something for everyone.

Taniti offers a wide ecosystem, from stunning beaches to the mountainous interior and the lush rain forest in between. Taniti even hosts a small but active volcano for the scientist in you!

At roughly 500 square miles taniti is a small island, but has plenty of activities to keep you busy. Whether you want to relax on the beach, hike through the jungle, or explore the local culture. Taniti is a safe island with plenty of ammenities to suit the needs of a wide array of personalities. Come visit and see for yourself why Taniti is the perfect destination for your next vacation!
