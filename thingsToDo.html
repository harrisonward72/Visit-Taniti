Beaches and Nature

Taniti is a nature lovers paradise. Come expirience our world renoun fluffy, white sandy beaches located in Yellow Leaf Bay. Or, if you're feeling adventurous, take the path less traveled and venture out to a volcanic rock beach.

Other tourists enjoy hiking through the lush tropical rainforests or, when its safe, sightseeing at our active volcano.

Until the recent increase of tourism, Tanitis economy relied heavily on fishing. There are a wide range of tropical fish in the waters surrounding Taniti and with the rise of tourism there are many companies that provide guided fishing charters. Snorkling is another activity many enjoy doing and is another way to expirience the many species of tropical fish. There are many companies that provide guided trips that can be easily located in Yellow Leaf Bay.

Feel like flying through the rainforest canopy? Great! Zip-lining is a new activity people are loving and is an exciting way to see nature.

Entertainment

Although Taniti is known for its natural attractions there is still plenty man-made attractions that are worth checking out!

Check out Taniti's historic museum to learn about the past of the island. Once you leave the museum you will be surrounded by several pubs and a microbrewery, if you're of age, you're welcome to come in and grab a drink.

Are you a fan of nightlife? Do you love to dance? Taniti has a dance club that is an absolute blast! If dancing isn't your thing you could hit up the arcade, go bowling or see a movie after a long day of fun in the sun.

Want to hit the links? Taniti will soon offer a small, but beautifully maintained 9-hole golf course on our north shore. The expected opening date will be next year, so be sure to mark your calander!

If you're interested in a birds eye view of the Island you can even take a private helicopter tour!

Taniti truly has something to offer for everyone from all walks of life! All are welcome! Most of these activities are in the Merriton Landing area, on the North side of Yellow Leaf Bay.
